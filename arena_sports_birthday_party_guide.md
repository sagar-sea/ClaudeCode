# Arena Sports Issaquah Birthday Party Guide

## Overview
Arena Sports Issaquah offers comprehensive birthday party packages that include escape rooms, arcade games, and private party rooms. This guide provides detailed information about their offerings, especially for large groups.

## Escape Room + Arcade Games Birthday Party Package

### Package Components
- **30 minutes**: Escape Room Activity
- **30 minutes**: Arcade Games
- **45 minutes**: Private Party Room (food, cake, presents)

### Escape Room Themes

#### 1. ZRL: Zombie Research Lab
- **Players**: 2-6
- **Difficulty**: Easy-Medium
- **Scenario**: Find a cure for the Zombyte virus before robo-zombies find you
- **Storyline**: As scientists trapped in a lab, your team must search for scientific equipment, solve virology puzzles, and manage resources while avoiding zombie encounters

#### 2. MAYDAY
- **Players**: 3-6
- **Difficulty**: Medium
- **Scenario**: Prevent a submarine from sinking by closing water-tight doors
- **Storyline**: Navigate through submarine sections, operate control panels, decode naval communications, and manage flooding containment

#### 3. SINGULARITY
- **Players**: 3-6
- **Difficulty**: Medium-Hard
- **Scenario**: Investigate a missing research vessel in deep space
- **Storyline**: Explore space research vessel, repair ship systems, decode scientific logs, and solve cosmic mysteries

## Handling Large Groups (18+ Kids)

### Official Capacity Limits
Each escape room has a maximum capacity:
- ZRL: Zombie Research Lab - 2-6 players
- MAYDAY - 3-6 players
- SINGULARITY - 3-6 players

### Arena Sports Solution for Large Groups

#### Multiple Concurrent Sessions
For a group of 18 kids:
- Run all three escape rooms simultaneously
- Split group into three teams of 6 kids each
- Each team experiences a different escape room theme at the same time

#### Party Host Coordination
- Dedicated party hosts manage group division
- Coordinate timing between activities
- Facilitate smooth transitions between escape rooms, arcade, and party room

#### Custom Package Arrangements
- Contact party coordinators directly for groups larger than standard
- Create custom arrangements with advance notice
- Special accommodations available for large birthday parties

### Recommended Booking Process
When booking for 18 kids, specifically ask:
1. How they handle groups of 18 kids for escape rooms
2. If all three rooms can run simultaneously
3. What happens if there are more or fewer children
4. Whether there's an additional charge for coordinating large groups

- Bring your own table decorations (tablecloths, centerpieces)

- All guests must complete waivers

- Legal guardian signature required for participants under 18

## Recommendations

### Before Booking
1. Confirm capacity arrangements for your specific group size
2. Discuss timing and transitions between activities
3. Inquire about customization options for your party
4. Ask about backup plans in case of scheduling conflicts

### During the Party
1. Arrive early to allow time for waiver completion if not done online
2. Have party host facilitate group divisions
3. Take advantage of photo opportunities throughout the activities
4. Communicate any special needs or concerns to your party coordinator
