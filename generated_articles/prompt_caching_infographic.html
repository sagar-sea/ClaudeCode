The Golden Rule of Prompt Caching
Only enable caching when you can guarantee a follow-up request within the 5-minute sliding TTL. Place all static content (system prompt, instructions, few-shot examples) at the exact top of the prefix — a single variable inserted before it breaks the entire prefix match and zeroes your hit rate. Audit your cached_tokens value in every API response.
